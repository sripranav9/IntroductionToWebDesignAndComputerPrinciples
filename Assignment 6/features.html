Features

Scale

Arth-E is a small-scale robot to mimic the small sizes of arthropods in the desert region.

Wheggs

The 3D printed Wheggs (Wheels+Legs) is one of the most important components of this robot.

Body

The body is laser cut to suit the tapering shape of arthropods. Thin wood is used as a material for the same.

Power Backup

The ~2200mAh battery supports the robot for about 2 hours of continuous testing.

Circuit

The circuitry along with the battery is soldered and it's mounted in a way that achieves good balance.

Fineness

Various 3D printers were used to test and achieve the best texture and quality of the wheels.
